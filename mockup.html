Padilla Mobile Mockup

This is an early prototype intended to help structure discussion. This prototype is best viewed in Google Chrome. The tool is aimed at criminal defense attorneys looking for resources regarding immigration consequences in accordance with Padilla. As such, the final product is intended as an interactive practice guide. It will also provide contact information for local immigration attorneys. The current logic is loosely based on Immigration Consequences of Massachusetts Criminal Convictions and would be refined further in the development process. Similarly, listings of attorneys are incomplete, and only provided here to help flesh out a prototype aimed at structuring discussion. The webapp includes three parts:

1. Guided Interview

Users are asked a series of branching questions to help determine relevant resources and potential courses of action.

2. Interview Summary Document

After the completion of the guided interview, users are presented with the option to 'save a summary of the interview for future reference. Currently, this takes the form of a MS Word file which the user can download. Future development may explore additional options such as the ability to email the summary.

3. Immigration Attorney Referral

The guided interview often suggests contacting an immigration attorney. To help facilitate this process, users can explore a directory of such attorneys based on location, either as seen on a map or as part of a list.
